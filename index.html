Our Story

How We Met

It started with a prompt that said, “if you’re a foodie, let’s taco about it” to which Greg suggested attending a baseball game. Greg suggested sitting in the center field section so “we” can catch a baseball. Illene told Greg “If by we you mean you, then yes. Greg Promised he would catch a baseball if it came flying at Ilene’s face.

Greg kept his promise and caught the baseball which secured a second date!

The Proposal

Ilene entered a chili cook off contest at work and won! On the same day, she also won an award at work. When she returned home from the office, she told Greg all about her wins and mentioned that she could have another title (a.k.a Mrs. Glinoga). Greg smiled and surprisingly agreed with Ilene…and pulled out a ring!

We are so excited to celebrate this next chapter in our lives with you, see you in July!
